Debut Taco Bell Trouble!! Yolo-kun's Debut!!

Bio

Swag chan is, of course, the main protagonist of the series. Originally manifested into existence (not born) by the witch queen, Swag chans purpose is to bring kek to the witches, as the kekkest witch. Swag chan is 47 (and a half) years old, and was in her late forties already when brought into the world (pretty young for a witch - they can live past 800! Plus she has an excellent skin care routine). Swag chan presents herself as being bold and confident, and can be quite cocky and stubborn. However, deep inside she has insecurities, something which she can be embarrassed to admit. Swag chan used to be vehemently against cringe, but is now much more accepting and will embrace things she previously considered cringe. All of Swag chans swag power is stored in her eyes, which is why she wears her sunglasses as the power is so immense that anybody who looks into her eyes will immediately die. Her best tomodachi is Yolo chan, who she met outside of a Taco Bell (which of course led to the infamous Taco Bell incident, causing Swag chan to be banned from every Taco Bell location in Texas). Swag chan is in love with Epic chan, who is also the only person who can look at Swag chans eyes as they are soulmates. Swag chan also has a fan club dedicated to her (and later on, also dedicated to Yolo chan). Her favorite food is ramen.

Bio

Smug chan is a witch who is Epic chans childhood best friend, when Epic chan was known as Majo Kawaii. They were roommates in Witch College until Majo Kawaii went missing. Several years later, while out on a walk, Smug chan accidentally found herself in a portapotty in the human world, where she would soon find her best tomodachi. Smug chan is indeed smug, and is pretty chilled out and laid back. She is banned from Tumblr and loves Funyuns. She tends to talk in her sleep, dreaming about eating more Funyuns or about Among Us. She has a fairy named Bruhbruh and is good friends with Matcha chan, with whom she played a 72 hour Yo Mama Kart marathon - without bathroom breaks.
